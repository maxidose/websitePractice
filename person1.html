About Me

Click to learn more about me.

Hey there! I'm Jefferson Valerozo Tabisola, A.K.A "Maxwell Harrington" born on June 12, 2004, in the province of Isabela, Philippines. My parents, Jennifer Tabisola and Sonia Valerozo, have always been my biggest supporters.

I started my education at Doña Concha Elementary School and later moved on to Monico Rarama National High School. Right now, I’m studying at Isabela State University, Roxas Campus, working towards a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. I’m also an NC2 passer in Organic and Agriculture Production under TESDA—something I’m really proud of!

When I’m not hitting the books, you’ll find me either coding or working on music. I love creating song covers and sharing them on Spotify—it’s a cool mix of my love for tech and music. I’m also a bit of an adventurer, always on the lookout for new experiences.

That’s a bit about me. I’m all about balancing my creative side with my tech skills, and I’m excited to see where life takes me next!
